Help moms achieve physical, emotional and financial health on their journey

We need to do more to support women's health and well-being as they navigate the path to parenthood, beginning even prior to conception. Workplace accomodations and HR benefits often determine if a woman has coverage for fertility services, if needed, and prenatal visits; how long they have to remain on the job prior to giving birth; if they have access to postnatal health care visits; and how quickly they may have to return to work after their baby is born. These decisions, for many, will impact their ability to create a family and thrive.
